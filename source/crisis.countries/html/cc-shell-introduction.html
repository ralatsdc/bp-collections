INTRODUCTION

Blu Pen's first series focuses on seven countries undergoing humanitarian crisis situations caused by factors such as natural disasters and political conflicts.

For each series, we search among the millions of internet sources by keywords, analyze thousands using factors such as community engagement and frequency of posts, and then collect the trusted content. We then take the content and display it in a sequence of four original d3 dynamic displays that you navigate visually. For further exploration you can see a sample of the source content. We have streamlined your finding, selecting, and organizing web content, to save you time reading the web content you care about.

For this seriries, Blu Pen's first, we invite you to make a comparison between the content related to popular culture and content related to seven countries in crisis situations. We hope you’ll discover new sources and delight in our surprising storehouses that prove ”the whole is other than the sum of its parts.“
