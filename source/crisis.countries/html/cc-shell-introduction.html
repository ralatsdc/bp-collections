INTRODUCTION

Blu Pen collects sources you can trust. We display a slice of their web content in beautiful visual collections stored for reading now or later.

For each series, we search among the millions of internet sources, analyze thousands, and provide you with the best. We streamline your finding, selecting, and organizing web content, to save you time reading the web content you care about.

We identify trusted sources by analyzing factors such as community interaction and frequency of posts, then collect the trusted content in original dynamic works that you navigate visually. If you can't read your collection now, you can read them later. There's no need to worry about missing something or being left behind.

You’ll discover new sources and delight in our surprising storehouses that prove ”the whole is other than the sum of its parts.“
