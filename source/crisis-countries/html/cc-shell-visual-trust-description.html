Trust

Readers Engage

Circles represent a trusted source color coded by the social media service, and the amount of community engagement with each source. More engagement implies more trust.

Trust is measured by a ratio. For Flickr, by the ratio of members to posts. For Tumblr, by the ratio of likes to posts. And for Twitter, by the ratio of followers to tweets.

More saturated colors indicate more trusted sources.

To view the name of the source, hover over a circle. To view a sampling of the content, click on a circle.
